Ethan Kho (valedictorian)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about Ethan Kho (valedictorian). For information on his work as an artist see eKho (artist).

Ethan Kho, valedictorian.

Ethan Vincei Tonio Kho (born March 16, 2001) is a college student from Sacramento, California. Ethan enrolled in the University of California, Irvine in the fall quarter of 2019. Since then he has been working on his electrical engineering degree. Before college, he was the valedictorian of his graduating class at Inderkum High School.

He is noted for being Inderkum's most talented valedictorian ever, despite claims from former prinical Dan Motherspaw about Ethan's history of making "dick moves".
