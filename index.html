eKho (artist)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

This article is about eKho (artist). For other information see Ethan Kho (valedictorian).

This is a blue pencil

eKho (born March 16, 2001) is an American singer, songwriter, and producer from Sacramento, California. He released his first album in December 2017. Since then eKho has released three more albums and an EP.

Artistry

Musical Style

eKho's music covers a vast number of genres, proving difficult to describe. At times his music is more electronic, drawing from the type of music he listened to growing up. Other times his music can have more of a pop influence. Throughout the years eKho has developed his own musical style that is unique to him.

Although widely loved by most who have encountered his music, eKho has a few critics. Most notably his music has been criticised for its "voice modulation", a devastating blow to his career.
